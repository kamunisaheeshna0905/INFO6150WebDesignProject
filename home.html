Top 5 Trending Books

LOST IN TIME

Author: A G Riddle

Genre: Science Fiction, Thriller

In Lost in Time, one of the inventors of the time travel machine confesses to the murder of the woman he loves, another of the six inventors, and is sent to the Triassic era of time where he has been exiled. In the meantime, his daughter is doing everything she can to get him back.
ONE OF US IS LYING

Author: Karen M. McManus

Genre: Mystery, Thriller

One of Us is Lying follows the gripping story of Bronwyn, Addy, Nate, and Cooper as suspects in the murder of Simon Kelleher. Each of the high school students have secrets that they would do anything to protect, so how far would they go to make sure they're kept out of the spotlight?
LIVE YOUR BEST LIE

Author: Jessie Weaver

Genre: Suspense, Adult Fiction

Influencer Summer Cartwright's glamorous life is cut short when she disappears during her Halloween party. A chilling social media post foretells her death, leading her friends to a dark discovery: Summer has been murdered, exposing a hidden reality behind her seemingly perfect online image
THE NIGHT LIGHT

Author: Nick Adcede

Genre: Horror

After three boys disappear in the woods with only one returning, claiming a "Night Light" took his friends to Hell, skeptical scientist Laura embarks on a quest to uncover the truth. Her investigation propels her into a terrifying confrontation with the unknown, questioning the limits of science and the reality of otherworldly realms.
STORY ABOUT YOU AND ME

Author: Pamela DuMond

Genre: Romance

Nineteen-year-old Sophie, battling MS, moves to L.A. seeking unconventional treatments. Keeping her condition a secret, she unexpectedly finds herself in peril but is saved by Alejandro, who becomes her driver and protector. Amidst the chaos of her health fight, they grow close, each harboring untold secrets, opening the door to unexpected love.
